Order Summary

How To Order

Currently we offer in online and in-person services. Just select the 'Order Online' tab, and browse our selection! Our system is designed for your convenience whether you're looking for sweets for special occasions or not. Feel free to contact us for any questions or concerns.

Online or In-person?

Online orders are quick and easy. We provide a customization option for cakes, as well as access to all of our other sweets. Please keep in mind custom orders must be submitted 2 weeks prior to event date.

In-person orders come with free consultants for advice and guidance from one of our professional bakers to ensure your vision is heard.

What do we offer?

Crumbz currently offers cakes, cookies, and cinnamon rolls. Our main focus is put on cake production, but much of our love is also put into the cookies and cinnamon rolls we offer! Everything is baked fresh daily.

We offer cakes with set designs, and customization cakes to your liking.

Please keep in mind that our cookies and cinnamon rolls are on a first come first serve basis.
